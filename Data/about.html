About RainOnWires

Hello, my name's Mikey. I'm the programmer and person behind IllusiveInterwebs.dev, I enjoy programming frequently if I am motivated enough. On a quiet night I happen to like to look at the stars and listen to indie music. Most of my creations are works of fiction, as I do write stories. Most of my works involve heavy subjects, or subjects that are hard to ponder upon. Personally, thinking outside the box is what I do best. With my liking of music, I have come to liking almost generally all genres. -Minus Pop-Music. (Not my thing. -Indie Pop, sure.) Realistically I often lean towards jazz music or slow songs. From time to time I enjoy banging it out to some metal. (Periphery is a band worth mentioning) I'm also not into politics, so please don't bring them up with me :) I consider myself not the political compass.
